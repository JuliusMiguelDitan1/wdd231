Islands & Itineraries PH – Site Plan

Site Name

Islands & Itineraries PH

This name highlights the Philippines’ 7,600+ islands while promising ready‑made travel routes (“itineraries”). The “PH” suffix instantly signals the geographic focus and helps the name feel like a recognizable brand handle or domain.

Sample available domain: islandsanditineraries.ph

Site Purpose

The site helps both local and international travelers decide where to go and how to experience each destination in the Philippines through concise reviews, curated recommendations, downloadable itineraries, and practical travel tips.

Key Audience Scenarios

  1. “Which Philippine islands are easiest to combine in a one‑week trip?”
  2. “What’s the best month to visit Siargao for surfing and avoid typhoons?”
  3. “Do you have a budget breakdown for backpacking Palawan?”

Color Scheme

#FFB300

Primary (Teal #00695C) – used for headings, links, and buttons to evoke tropical seas.
Accent (Sunrise Gold #FFB300) – used for callouts, icons, and underline accents to suggest warm sunlight.

Typography

  • Montserrat (600/800) – headings & navigation.
  • Open Sans (400/600) – body copy, captions, UI labels.
